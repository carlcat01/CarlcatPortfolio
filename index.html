Making a CNC Machine

After a year of 3d printing my ideas, I was ready to take my designs to the next step. Using the rough design from V1 Enginnering, I built my own CNC mill. My design has an usable area of 230x280x110mm. It uses Repatier firmware, mainly because I am familiar with it from my 3d printer.

The first mill, done with foam so nothing breaks.

The powersupply and installed RAMPS board.

The RAMPS board

It took a while for the design to come together, mainly since printing took a bit.

4 Horizontal, and 2 vertical rails allow the working end to move along the X, Y, and Z planes.

An overview of the finished assembly.
